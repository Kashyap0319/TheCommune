Kanak Murarka

Founder, The Commun

Meet Kanak Murarka

Kanak started The Commun after noticing a very real problem — students moving to Mumbai for NMIMS had no reliable, student-friendly source for finding PGs and flats. Most brokers were either too expensive, too slow, or simply didn't understand student needs.

She built The Commun to fill that gap. With deep knowledge of the Vile Parle and Andheri micro-markets, Kanak personally reviews every listing before sharing it with students.

Today, The Commun has helped 500+ students find their home near campus — with zero drama and complete transparency.
