Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

The Commun ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website, WhatsApp chatbot, Instagram messaging service, or any related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, and WhatsApp number you provide via our lead form or messaging services.
  • Housing Preferences: Preferred area, budget, accommodation type, and move-in date.
  • Messaging Data: Messages you send to us via WhatsApp or Instagram DMs for the purpose of responding to your enquiry.
  • Usage Data: Basic website analytics such as page views (no personal tracking).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Match you with suitable PG and flat listings near NMIMS.
  • Respond to your enquiries via WhatsApp or Instagram.
  • Send you property options and photos based on your preferences.
  • Create leads in our CRM system to manage follow-ups.
  • Improve our services and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information with:

  • Property owners/landlords — only the minimum info needed to arrange a viewing.
  • Service providers (Meta/WhatsApp, Zoho CRM, Google) that help us operate our platform, subject to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard services (Zoho CRM, Google Sheets). We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Instagram & WhatsApp Messaging

When you message us on Instagram or WhatsApp, your messages are processed by our automated chatbot to provide quick responses. Messages are used only to assist with your property search and are not used for any other purpose. You can stop the conversation at any time by simply not responding.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at snehach077@gmail.com.

7. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at snehach077@gmail.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of the request.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
